The Process

From Bare Land to Four Revenue Streams

CLT manages every step — planting, irrigation, registry certification, harvest coordination, and credit sales. Partners provide land or capital. CLT provides everything else.

01
Site Assessment & Agreement
CLT evaluates land characteristics, soil, water depth, and sun exposure. Deal structure, revenue share, and all rights to the four revenue streams are finalized before a single tree goes in the ground.
02
Planting & Establishment
Phoenix Trees are planted at optimized density. Initial irrigation is active for Years 0–2. Carbon sequestration and registry baseline measurements begin immediately on Day 1.
03
Annual Carbon Credit Issuance
Each year, CLT submits verified sequestration data to registered VCM registries. Credits are certified, serialized, and made available for sale. Revenue distributed per agreement.
04
Harvest, Graphene & Regeneration
At maturity, CLT coordinates harvest of dense Phoenix hardwood. Lumber sold at premium rates. A portion goes through the combustion/graphene pathway. Trees regenerate. Cycle repeats up to 15 times.
